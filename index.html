Q&A about avocado

What is an avocado?

An avocado is a nutrient-rich fruit with creamy flesh, native to Central and South America.

Are avocados healthy?

Yes, avocados are packed with healthy fats, vitamins, and fiber, making them a great choice for a balanced diet.

How do you store avocados?

Store unripe avocados at room temperature, and once ripe, keep them in the fridge to prolong freshness.

How do you know if an avocado is ripe?

A ripe avocado is slightly soft to the touch but not mushy. Its skin may also darken slightly.

What can you make with avocados?

Avocados are versatile and can be used in guacamole, salads, toast, smoothies, and even desserts.
